Privacy Policy — Medilingo

Effective date: October 14, 2025. This Privacy Policy explains how Medilingo collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Introduction

Medilingo ("we", "our", or "the App") is a medical translation and learning application for Android users. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your information responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: full name, username, email address, and profile data when you register.
  • Authentication data: login credentials (securely stored), tokens, and session information.
  • Translation data: text you upload or type, translation history, detected languages.
  • Usage data: screens visited, buttons clicked, preferences, saved summaries, and activity logs.
  • Device & technical data: device model, Android version, IP address, crash logs, and performance data.
  • Feedback: messages, ratings, and support requests you submit through the feedback form.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve Medilingo and its features.
  • Enable text translation, OCR text extraction, and summary generation features.
  • Personalize your experience (e.g., recent translations, saved history).
  • Communicate with you regarding your account and support requests.
  • Analyze performance, fix bugs, and improve stability.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.

4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only under these conditions:

  • Service providers: hosting providers, analytics platforms, and email delivery tools.
  • Legal requirements: when required by law, regulation, or a court order.
  • Business transitions: during mergers, acquisitions, or transfers (with prior notice if required).
  • With your consent: when you explicitly request your data to be shared.

5. Third-Party Services & Links

Medilingo may integrate with third-party services such as AI APIs, analytics, and crash reporting tools. These services handle data according to their own privacy policies. The App may contain external links; we are not responsible for third-party privacy practices.

6. Analytics and Cookies

We may use analytics to understand how users interact with Medilingo. We do not use cookies in the Android app, but we may store small local data to save app preferences.

7. How We Store and Secure Your Data

We use secure servers, HTTPS encryption, and access controls to protect your data. While we take strong measures, no system is completely secure. If a security incident occurs, we will notify affected users as required by law.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as needed to operate the App or as required by law. You may delete your account at any time to remove profile and translation data from our servers.

9. Children's Privacy

Medilingo is not intended for children under 13 (or a higher age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Your Rights and Choices

You may have rights depending on your region, including:

  • Accessing your personal data.
  • Requesting correction or deletion.
  • Requesting a copy of your data.
  • Restricting certain data processing activities.

For any such requests, please contact us. We will respond in accordance with applicable laws.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed or stored in countries outside your own. When required, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Major changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or email. The "Effective date" above shows the latest update.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions or privacy concerns, you may contact us:

  • Email: medilingo.support@gmail.com
  • From the App: Go to Settings → Help & Feedback.

Thank you for using Medilingo. We value your trust and privacy.
